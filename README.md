# Vidya Bridge - Educational Sponsorship Platform

**Vidya Bridge** is an educational sponsorship platform that connects donors with children in vulnerable situations in India, ensuring complete transparency through blockchain technology.

## 🎯 What is Vidya Bridge?

Vidya Bridge (meaning "bridge of knowledge" in Sanskrit) is an educational sponsorship system that:

- **Protects children's privacy** using unique IDs instead of real names
- **Ensures transparency** with all donations recorded on blockchain
- **Uses stable currency (USDC)** to avoid volatility
- **Shows only region** (not specific location) to protect minors
- **Enables direct donations** without intermediaries

## 🛡️ Privacy Protection

- ✅ **Unique IDs** instead of real names (e.g., IND-STU-001)
- ✅ **Region only** visible (e.g., "Northern India")
- ✅ **No photos** or personal information
- ✅ **Clear educational goals** that are verifiable

## 💰 Donation System

- **Currency**: USDC (stable, no volatility)
- **Transparency**: All donations on blockchain
- **Direct**: No intermediaries, funds go directly to the student
- **Verifiable**: Every transaction is public and auditable

## 🌍 Social Impact

Vidya Bridge is designed to expand to other countries, always maintaining:
- Protection of minors' privacy
- Complete fund transparency
- Direct donations without intermediaries
- Blockchain verification of every transaction

## 🎯 Key Features

- **Complete Privacy**: Full protection of minors' identity
- **Blockchain Transparency**: All transactions are public and verifiable
- **Stable Currency**: USDC to avoid price volatility
- **Direct Donations**: No intermediaries, funds go directly to students
- **Global Scalability**: Designed to expand to other countries
